I've kept these documents: they are the last page of his diary and the reports that were sent to the company he worked for while he was on assignment to the "Client".<br />

Once the retraining course was over, Master Canaro was never the same again.<br />
He went back to his company, but stayed only long enough to hand in his resignation.<br />
He founded our Order and began preaching the mysticism of programming, immediately gaining a large following.<br />
The time was ripe for change: there had been the prodigious growth of the end of the millennium, and there had been the ruinous crash of March 11, 2000.
